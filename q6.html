Recipe: Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 3 tbsp butter
  • 1 cup tomato purée
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander for garnish

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, red chili powder, turmeric, and salt. Let it sit for at least 2 hours.
  2. Heat butter in a pan and cook the marinated chicken until tender. Remove and set aside.
  3. In the same pan, add tomato purée, red chili powder, and garam masala. Cook for 10 minutes.
  4. Add the cooked chicken and fresh cream to the pan. Simmer for another 10 minutes.
  5. Garnish with fresh coriander and serve hot with naan or rice.

Nutritional Information

  • Calories: 490 per serving
  • Protein: 30g
  • Carbohydrates: 10g
  • Fats: 35g

Fun Fact

Butter Chicken originated in Delhi in the 1950s at the famous Moti Mahal restaurant!
